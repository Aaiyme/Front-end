ABOUT MUSASHI MIYAMOTO

Miyamoto Musashi (宮みや本もと武む蔵さし, Miyamoto Musashi) is one
of the main protagonists in Vagabond,and a historical figure considered one
of the greatest Samurai (swordsmen) in Japanese history. As a young man he
embarks on a journey to become the strongest samurai after being exiled from
his village.

He was born under the name Takezou Shinmen (新しん免めん 武たけ蔵ぞう,
Shinmen Takezo) but later changed it to Musashi Miyamoto.
